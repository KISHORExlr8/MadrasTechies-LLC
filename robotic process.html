Efficiency through automation of the mundane

In many instances, the smooth day-to-day operations of businesses hinge on the execution of monotonous tasks, often carried out manually. At Madras Techies, we offer Robotic Process Automation solutions to assist businesses in automating repetitive, rule-based, or trigger-driven tasks. Through RPA,

  • Minimize Errors
  • Enhance Productivity
  • Increase Employee Satisfaction
  • Facilitate Accelerated Transformation

Our team of RPA experts identifies bottleneck tasks within your workflow and delegates them to a digital workforce. As a result, operational efficiency improves, enabling your human resources to focus on more meaningful tasks.

Robotic Process Automation (RPA) Services

RPA Consultancy

Our team of specialists will comprehend your business workflow to evaluate your readiness for RPA, identify potential obstacles, and assess automation possibilities, culminating in the creation of an RPA adoption roadmap.

Automation of IT Processes

Reduce time and costs while enhancing the accuracy and consistency of IT processes. Automate repetitive tasks in services and operations, and efficiently detect and respond to security threats.

Automation of Business Processes

Elevate user and customer experiences through business process automation. Automate HR, finance, and insurance processes, excelling in customer experience management with RPA.

RPA Proof Of Concept

Aligned with the automation roadmap, a Proof of Concept (PoC) for RPA is developed on a small scale to showcase the Return on Investment (RoI) in process transformation, justifying enterprise-wide RPA implementation.

Managed RPA Services

Direct your focus on the automation pipeline, eliminating bottlenecks in your workflow with RPA automation. Entrust the maintenance and support tasks to our certified RPA professionals.

Support

Ensure consistent optimal performance of all implemented RPA processes. Our support team, comprising experts and automated monitoring systems, is available 24/7 to assist you.

RPA Implementation Methodology

Assessment

Evaluate the business's RPA readiness by analyzing the existing workflow, data, and technology. Develop a customized pipeline for the implementation of tailored RPA solutions.

Process Mining

Utilize this technique to pinpoint bottlenecks or inefficiencies in end-to-end processes, ensuring their inclusion in the automation pipeline.

Implementation

Develop and implement RPA solutions into your workflow based on the established automation pipeline. Involve your in-house team to acquaint them with the intricacies of automation.

Training

Madras Techies will cultivate your employees' RPA proficiency from the ground up. Our experts will provide guidance and training for the effective development and deployment of RPA in your organization.

Centre of Excellence

Our subsequent step involves strategizing the establishment of a Center of Excellence (CoE) to deeply integrate RPA into your system, ensuring the future of your automation is in capable and expert hands.

Support

Beyond mere implementation, Madras Techies prioritizes the consistent performance of your automation. Our team of experts and automated monitoring systems are dedicated to enhancing incident management response.
